About

NorthWest Scuba Diving was established in 2020 on the beautiful northwest coast of Ireland. The 5 star dive center is located in Mullaghmore, Co.Sligo and is perfectly positioned for divers to experience the beautiful underwater world of the North-West Atlantic.

Divers have the opportunity to explore the stunning natural wonders just off the shore from Mullaghmore, take a short boat ride around the coast to dive BallyConnell, or head across Donegal bay to experience the wonders of the many spectacular dives the coast of Donegal has to offer.
